BRUNSON'S PUB: ST. PAUL'S NEIGHBORHOOD FAVORITE

Overview
Brunson's Pub is a welcoming neighborhood restaurant and bar located on St. Paul's East Side. Since opening in 2017, the establishment has become a go-to spot for quality comfort food, craft cocktails, and good times on Payne Avenue.

Location & Hours
Address: 956 Payne Ave, St. Paul, MN 55130
Free parking available on-site.

Regular Hours:
- Sunday to Thursday: 11am - 11pm (Kitchen: 11am - 10pm)
- Friday & Saturday: 11am - 12 midnight (Kitchen: 11am - 11pm)

Brunch Service:
- Every Saturday & Sunday: 11am - 2pm
- Full menu also available during brunch hours

Cuisine & Menu Style
Brunson's serves scratch-made comfort food with a creative touch. Chef Torrance Beavers brings culinary expertise to classic American fare, creating approachable yet flavorful dishes. The menu includes burgers, sandwiches, salads, and shareables.

Signature Dishes & Menu Highlights
- Scratch-made burgers with creative toppings and flavor combinations
- Po' Boy sandwiches (Catfish Po Boy is a rotating special)
- House-made dressings and sauces
- Wedge salad with smoked blue cheese, red onion, tomatoes, avocado, and bacon
- Add-ons include grilled chicken, brisket, or seared yellowfin tuna
- Weekly Chef's features (new specials every Wednesday)

Beverage Program
The pub features 19 taps with rotating craft beers, creative cocktails, and a curated wine list. Local breweries like Summit and Schell's are regularly featured.

Special Events & Weekly Features
Tuesday: Trivia Mafia at 8:30pm with Schell's & Summit specials starting at 8:30pm, Late Night Happy Hour at 9pm.
Wednesday: Chef's Weekly Feature (new dish each week), Brunson's Wednesday Wing (one-day-only flavor drop - peppadew pepper jam, peanuts, and green onion variations), Wine & Whiskey Wednesday with rotating pours.
Sunday: Chopped Cheese Sundays (Harlem-inspired, St. Paul-perfected) available after 4pm.
Weekend: Brunch service with Lovejoy's Bloodies, fresh-squeezed mimosas, and scratch-made classics.

(Food specials run until kitchen closes; drink specials continue through late night hours)

What Makes It Special
Brunson's combines quality food with neighborhood hospitality. Chef Torrance Beavers' creative touch elevates traditional comfort food beyond standard pub fare. The rotating weekly features and specials keep the menu fresh. The East Side location is convenient to Metro State and features authentic neighborhood character.
