EASTSIDE ST. PAUL RESTAURANTS: A GUIDE FOR METRO STATE STUDENTS

Understanding the East Side Food Scene
The East Side of St. Paul has become a culinary hotspot with diverse dining options ranging from casual neighborhood spots to upscale restaurants. This area is particularly accessible to Metro State University students and features authentic, community-rooted establishments.

What Makes East Side Special
The neighborhood combines historic character with contemporary culinary creativity. You'll find family-owned businesses serving traditional recipes alongside innovative restaurants pushing culinary boundaries. The area maintains authentic neighborhood feeling while attracting broader food lovers.
