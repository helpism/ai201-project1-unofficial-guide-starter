SWEDE HOLLOW CAFE: ST. PAUL'S NEIGHBORHOOD GATHERING PLACE SINCE 1997

Overview
Swede Hollow Cafe is a beloved neighborhood institution that has been serving the St. Paul community for nearly 30 years. Built on the foundation of genuine community connection, this cafe is where East Side locals gather for quality food and meaningful social interaction.

Location
Address: 725 E 7th St, St. Paul, MN 55106
Heart of the neighborhood with easy access from Metro State area

Operating Hours
Monday - Sunday: 9:00am - 2:00pm
(Closed Mondays on holidays like Memorial Day)

The Swede Hollow Philosophy
What started as a neighborhood cafe evolved into something much more significant. Many customers are known by name, and their chef's creations are eagerly anticipated each morning and afternoon. The cafe focuses on building genuine community connections through food.
