TONGUE IN CHEEK: ST. PAUL'S FINE DINING DESTINATION

Overview
Tongue in Cheek (TIC) is an eating, drinking, and cavorting establishment that brings upscale dining to St. Paul. With a focus on humane sourcing and quality ingredients, the restaurant offers a refined experience for those seeking elevated cuisine and craft beverages.

Dining Philosophy
The restaurant emphasizes ethical sourcing and sustainable practices:
- Certified Humane commitment
- Eco-friendly ingredient sourcing
- Wild-caught and humanely-raised proteins
- Support for sustainable farming practices

They partner with respected organizations including Certified Humane, Humane Farming Association, and EatWild to ensure ingredient integrity.
